norma mitica thesis project

2024

norma mitica is a proposal for a new academic discipline of design, a printed product, and a website that questions new socio-cultural needs. what is the role of the designer in contemporary society? this thesis questions how design today can be anti-colonialist, anti-racist, non-sexist, non-ableist, feminist, non-binary, and queer. to become aware of one's privileges and design a future without any forms of oppression.
