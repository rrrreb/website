scomodo

2019 - ongoing

scomodo is an under30 organization founded in Rome in 2016, with the aim of discussing local issues and creating a magazine focusing on current affairs and politics. initially famous for its “notti scomode”, which involved overnight occupations to draw attention to abandoned places in rome and bring them back to life for a night. since then, the company has grown. today, it has a staff of over 30 people, including editors, graphic designers, finance and events departments,and four locations in italy: rome, milan, empoli, and bari.
